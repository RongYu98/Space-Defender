Space Defender:

Intro: This will be a classic alien space shooting game.

Guidelines:
I. User will be able to control a ship and shoot using that ship.
    A. Will make a ship class and a bullet class.
II. There will be enemies appearing in simple formation, and they will randomly shoot.
    A. Make hit functions.
III. Define a boarder for all movement.
IV. Powerups will be dropped.
    A. Frequency of shots.
    B. Piercing of shots.
    C. Speed increase.
    D. Invulnerbility. 
V. Enemies will come in differently and move differently.
    A. Divides into rows and the rows move in different directions.
    B. Random columns will move foward with the intent to crash into the user.
